Frequently Asked Questions

Please reach us at if you cannot find an answer to your question.

What is Women Into Cybersecurity?

Women into Cybersecurity Society is a community dedicated to empowering women in STEM, particularly in the field of cybersecurity. We provide resources, events, and support to foster a more inclusive environment.

How do I become a member of Women Into Cybersecurity?

To become a member, simply visit our membership page and follow the registration instructions. It's quick and easy!

Are events open to all skill levels?

Yes, our events cater to a range of skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, there's something for everyone.

How can I suggest an event or workshop idea?

We welcome suggestions! Feel free to submit your ideas here and our team will review them for consideration.

How does the mentorship program work?

Our mentorship program pairs experienced professionals with those looking for guidance. If you're interested in participating, fill out our mentorship application.

How can I contact the society for general inquiries?

You can reach out to us via email at info.wicyber@gmail.com. For more contact options, visit our Get in Touch page.

Is there a fee to join the society?

No, membership is free! We believe in providing accessible resources and opportunities to all our members.
